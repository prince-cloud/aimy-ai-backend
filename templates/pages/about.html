About Aimy AI

Your Intelligent Academic Assistant for KNUST AIM APP

Our Mission

Aimy AI is designed to revolutionize the academic experience for students at KNUST (Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology) through intelligent AI-powered assistance and personalized learning support.

We believe that every student deserves access to cutting-edge technology that can enhance their learning journey, track their progress, and provide personalized recommendations to help them achieve their academic goals.
